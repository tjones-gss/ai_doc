> **TL;DR** — Exports articles from Helpjuice into Word or PDF using the official GSS letterhead. Lets you turn an online doc into a polished, shareable file customers (and execs) recognize.

## Overview

Helpjuice is great for searchable web docs, but sometimes you need a physical-feeling artifact — a Word doc to send a customer, a PDF to attach to a contract, a handout for a training. gsshelp-letterpress takes a Helpjuice article, applies the GSS standard letterhead and formatting, and emits Word or PDF.

## Why use it

- **Branded output.** No more hand-formatting Helpjuice exports to look right.
- **Word + PDF in one tool.** Pick your format.
- **Consistent.** Every export follows the same letterhead style.

## When to use it

- A customer asks for a doc by email and you want it on letterhead.
- You're preparing handouts or printed material for training.
- You're attaching a doc to a formal communication.

Don't use it for:
- Quick internal sharing — just send the Helpjuice link.

## How to access it

- **URL:** [https://launchpad.globalshopsolutions.dev/apps/gsshelp-letterpress](https://launchpad.globalshopsolutions.dev/apps/gsshelp-letterpress) → **Launch**.
- **Login:** Global Shop Office 365 SSO.
- **Status:** Live.

## How to use it

1. Launch the app.
2. Paste the Helpjuice article URL or article ID.
3. Pick your output format (Word / PDF).
4. Download the result.

## Common questions

**Does it preserve images and tables?**
Yes — the export keeps Helpjuice's content layout and substitutes the letterhead frame.

**Can I customize the letterhead?**
The default uses the GSS standard. Confirm with the owner if you need a variant.
