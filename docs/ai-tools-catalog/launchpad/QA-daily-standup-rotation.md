> **TL;DR** — Picks who runs QA standup each day. Removes the awkward "uh, who's leading today?" moment. Just opens the page, see whose turn it is.

## Overview

A small but useful tool for the QA team's daily standup ritual. The app maintains a rotation of QA staff and shows whose turn it is to lead the meeting today. It handles skips (PTO, sick days) so the rotation stays fair.

## Why use it

- **No more "you go," "no, you go" at the start of standup.**
- **Visible and fair rotation.** Everyone takes a turn.
- **Fewer Outlook reminders to set.**

## When to use it

- Right before QA standup — open the page and see who's leading.
- When someone's out and you need to skip them in the rotation.

## How to access it

- **URL:** [https://launchpad.globalshopsolutions.dev/apps/QA-daily-standup-rotation](https://launchpad.globalshopsolutions.dev/apps/QA-daily-standup-rotation) → **Launch**.
- **Login:** Global Shop Office 365 SSO.
- **Status:** Live.
- **Access:** QA team.

## How to use it

1. Open the app at standup time.
2. Today's leader is shown.
3. Mark "skip" if they're out — rotation continues.
