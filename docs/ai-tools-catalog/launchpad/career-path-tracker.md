# career-path-tracker

> **TL;DR** — Track your career path through quarters. Log skills, achievements, and growth markers; export your data for review conversations and self-assessment.

## Overview

Career growth at GSS happens quarter by quarter, but it's easy to lose track of what you actually did. career-path-tracker gives you a structured place to log skills you've picked up, achievements you've shipped, and trajectory you're trying to build. Then, when review season hits, you can export your data instead of trying to reconstruct nine months of work from Outlook search.

## Why use it

- **No more "what did I do this quarter?" panic.** A running log replaces a frantic January reconstruction.
- **Structured growth conversation.** Bring concrete data to your manager 1:1 or career-path discussion.
- **Owns your own narrative.** Even if you change roles, your log goes with you.

## When to use it

- End of week / end of month — log notable skills, projects, achievements.
- Before a 1:1 — pull recent entries to anchor the conversation.
- Performance review prep — export and summarize.

Don't use it for:
- Anything that needs to be in your manager's tooling — this is your personal record.

## How to use it

1. Launch the app.
2. Pick the current quarter.
3. Log entries — skills picked up, achievements, key projects.
4. Export when needed (CSV / formatted summary).

## Common questions

**Is my data private?**
Your entries are yours. Confirm visibility settings on the app page.

**Can my manager see it?**
Only if you share the export with them.
